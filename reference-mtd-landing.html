The Problem

MTD Changes Everything.
Here's What It Actually Means.

The annual self assessment return you've been filing? It's being replaced. And it's happening whether you're ready or not.

📋

Quarterly Submissions — 4× Per Year

Every three months, a summary of your income and expenses must reach HMRC. Miss one deadline and penalties apply immediately — no grace period.

💻

Digital Records — Mandatory

Paper records and spreadsheets alone are no longer compliant. All income and expenses must be kept in HMRC-approved software from day one.

📅

5 Filings Per Year Minimum

4 quarterly updates, plus an End of Period Statement, plus a Final Declaration. That's 5× more admin than the single SA return you're used to.

⚠️

Penalties Start Immediately

Unlike the old SA system, HMRC's penalty clock starts ticking from 6 April 2026 — not at the end of the tax year. Early non-compliance is expensive.

FAQ

Common Questions.

Am I definitely affected by MTD ITSA?

If your gross income from self-employment and/or property exceeded £50,000 in the 2024/25 tax year, yes — MTD ITSA is mandatory for you from 6 April 2026. This is gross income (turnover), not profit. PAYE/umbrella income doesn't count toward the threshold, but CIS earnings and self-employment income do.

I already have an accountant. Can I still use Genius Money for MTD?

Yes. Many contractors use Genius Money for MTD compliance while retaining their existing accountant for company accounts or investment advice. We handle the quarterly updates and HMRC submissions — your accountant continues with everything else.

What do I need to do each quarter?

Very little. Three weeks before each deadline we send a reminder. You upload your receipts and invoices to our secure portal. We categorise, reconcile, and submit. Your total time per quarter: under 30 minutes.

What if I sign up after April 6?

You can still sign up after April 6 and we'll help you catch up — but HMRC penalties start from the first missed deadline. Signing up before April 1 also means your onboarding fee is waived (saving £95).

Is my limited company affected?

MTD for Corporation Tax is still under consultation — your limited company is not currently affected. However, if you also have personal self-employment or property income over £50,000, your personal tax position IS affected. Our Director tier handles PSC directors with mixed income.

How do you get authorised to file on my behalf?

We're an authorised HMRC tax agent. Once you sign up, we guide you through the HMRC Online Agent Authorisation — it takes about 10 minutes. HMRC processes this in 10–14 days, which is why signing up now is important.
