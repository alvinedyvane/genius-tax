1. Who We Are

For the purposes of UK data protection legislation (UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018), the data controller is Genius Workforce Solutions Limited.

If you have any questions about how we handle your personal data, please contact us at: hello@geniusmoney.co.uk

2. What Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following personal data:

  • Identity data: your name, date of birth, National Insurance number, UTR (Unique Taxpayer Reference)
  • Contact data: email address, telephone number, postal address
  • Financial data: income details, expenses, tax information, payment card details (processed securely via Stripe — we do not store card data)
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, referring URLs
  • Communications: any correspondence you send to us, including enquiries and support requests

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide our Making Tax Digital (MTD) compliance service and digital record-keeping
  • To submit quarterly updates and end-of-period statements to HMRC on your behalf
  • To manage your account and process payments
  • To send you service-related communications (e.g. submission confirmations, deadline reminders)
  • To respond to enquiries and provide customer support
  • To comply with our legal and regulatory obligations
  • To improve our website and services

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We rely on the following legal bases to process your personal data:

  • Contract: processing is necessary to perform our service agreement with you
  • Legal obligation: we must process certain data to comply with tax and regulatory requirements
  • Legitimate interests: for service improvement, fraud prevention and security
  • Consent: where we ask for your consent (e.g. marketing communications)

5. HMRC and Third-Party Sharing

As an HMRC Authorised Agent, we submit tax data to HMRC on your behalf. This is a core function of our service and is carried out under your instruction.

We may also share data with carefully selected third parties who assist in delivering our service, including:

  • Sage Software — our licensed accounting and MTD-compliant software provider
  • Stripe — secure payment processing
  • Cloud hosting providers — for secure data storage and service delivery

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. We do not use your data for any purpose other than those described in this policy.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, including meeting legal, accounting or reporting requirements. In practice, financial and tax records are typically retained for a minimum of six years in line with HMRC guidance.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights in relation to your personal data:

  • Right of access — to request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — to ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — to ask us to delete your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to restriction — to ask us to restrict how we use your data
  • Right to portability — to receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object — to object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at hello@geniusmoney.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.
